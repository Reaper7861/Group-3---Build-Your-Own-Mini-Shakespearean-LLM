Prompt: Come, cousin, canst thou quake,
Come, cousin, canst thou quake, and pale thy cheek
Before the shadow of a rising storm?

Prompt: O Romeo, Romeo! wherefore art thou
O Romeo, Romeo! wherefore art thou Romeo? Deny thy father and refuse thy name,
Or I'll no longer be a Capulet.

Prompt: Now is the winter of our discontent
Now is the winter of our discontent turned to gold
By the sweet breath of this victorious dawn.

Prompt: To be or none or little;
To be or none or little; 'tis the choice
Of fragile souls that fear the heavy weight
Of mortal crown and destiny.
